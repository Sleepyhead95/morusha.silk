Who is Morusha?

My name is Barbara Westport and I am the founder of Morusha Silk.

I learned the craft of silk painting from my mother, who was an artist. Over the years, I perfected the craft, mostly as a hobby, until my friends and family encouraged me to turn it into a business.

In 2018, I founded Morusha Silk, and the company has been growing steadily. Today, I have three other artisans working with me, whose skills I deeply cherish.

I personally make all the designs, and every inch of silk is hand-painted — a long, painstaking process whose fruits are much worth it. Despite the challenges, we continue to show up and we hope you will enjoy our products.
